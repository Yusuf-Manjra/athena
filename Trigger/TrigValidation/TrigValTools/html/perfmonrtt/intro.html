Trigger RTT Perfmon Results

This page gives an overview over the Trigger RTT results. Select a package of interest in the top navitation panel. At the bottom of each page you will find a legend of the symbols used.

The tuple in each cell (e.g. 1332M/8.9k) is the result of a linear fit to the vmem/event graph, i.e. the first number is the initial virtual memory consumption and the second number the memory increase per event. Left-click on the links for more options.

Your package and build selection on the pages will be remembered and re-applied on your next visit.
